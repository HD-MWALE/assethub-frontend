# AssetHub Brand Transformation

## From Studio Admin to AssetHub

A complete rebranding transformation that repositions the application as a comprehensive asset management system with a cohesive visual identity.

## Visual Transformation

### Logo Update
**Before**: Generic command icon
**After**: AssetHub hexagonal badge with navy and cyan colors
- Modern, tech-forward design
- Clear asset management association
- Scalable and recognizable

### Color Scheme
**Before**: Generic purple-based template colors
**After**: Professional Navy Blue (#001F3F) and Cyan (#00BCD4) palette
- Navy Blue: Trust, stability, professionalism
- Cyan: Technology, connectivity, asset tracking
- High contrast accessibility
- Optimized for light and dark modes

## Messaging Evolution

### Application Name
- **Before**: "Studio Admin"
- **After**: "AssetHub"

### Tagline
- **Before**: "Design. Build. Launch. Repeat."
- **After**: "Asset Management Made Simple"

### Key Messaging
| Page | Before | After |
|------|--------|-------|

## Asset Management Focus

Every element of the interface now speaks to asset management:

- **Terminology**: Assets, locations, assignments, maintenance
- **Metrics**: Tracked asset counts, maintenance schedules, assignment status
- **Navigation**: Asset-focused menu items
- **Messaging**: Value propositions around asset tracking and management
- **Visual Identity**: Hexagonal network pattern representing asset connectivity

## Maintaining Brand Consistency

When adding new features:
1. Use Navy Blue for primary actions
2. Use Cyan for highlights and accents
3. Reference asset management concepts in copy
4. Maintain the hexagonal badge in branding
5. Keep messaging professional yet accessible
6. Follow the established color palette
